Delete Your JuaMtaa Account

JuaMtaa users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data by contacting support from the email address used for the account.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email labcoatsxd@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: JuaMtaa account deletion request.
  3. Include the email address and phone number linked to the account.
Email Support

What We Delete

We delete account profile details and personal data that is no longer needed to provide JuaMtaa. Some booking, payment, safety, fraud prevention, or legal records may be retained where required by law or necessary to resolve disputes and protect users.

Timing

We aim to review deletion requests within 30 days. We may contact you to verify account ownership before completing the request.
